We Plan To:

Collaborate with companies from an organization and sell the items that our warehouse creates after using those recycled trash.
After preparing the recycled materials, we send it off to companies or people that may want it.
Help solve the problem of the growing trash happening in this world. We would like to recycle this trash into materials that make up our daily lives.
Plastic would be turned into furniture, shoes, and toys.
Paper would be turned into toilet paper, napkins, cardboard, tissue paper, and handbags.
Metal would be turned into home appliances, furniture, automobiles, hospital equipment.
